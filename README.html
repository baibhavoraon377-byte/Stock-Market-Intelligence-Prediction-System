Data Source & Rate Limits

Live prices are fetched via yfinance which wraps Yahoo Finance's unofficial API. A 0.35 s throttle is applied between requests to avoid rate limits. Cached data refreshes every 60 seconds for quotes and 5 minutes for ML training sets.

💡

Tips for Best Results

Use .NS suffix for NSE-India stocks (e.g. RELIANCE.NS), -USD for crypto (e.g. BTC-USD). For ML predictions, select at least 2y of training data for meaningful accuracy. Backtesting works best with periods of 2y+.
